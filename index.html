Campus Life

School Playground

was established when the school was first built.

The school playground is a great place to play with friends. You can play many games like Hide and Seek, tag, Freeze tag, and many other games!



School Auditorium

was also built in when the school was first built.

The School Auditorium is a great place to have anouncements and perform talent shows on the stage.



School Track

was built a few years after the school was built.

The school track is a great place to run and get some excersize. It is very fun to run on the track and you can even play ootball in the middle area.
